# roadmap/ — ought × action (direction · milestones) guide
> **Grid:** ought × action. **Permission:** Schema. **Change velocity:** slow.
> **Parent role:** the macro-direction parent of plan (tactical planning) and sprint (work).
> Lives under `project/` because it is the future extension of identity, not an independent pillar.

## Goes in: milestones (phase), stage-transition gates, "where we ought to go."
## Stays out: detailed planning (→ .union-stack/plan), current work (→ .union-stack/sprint).
## Files: PHASE-* (ZFS naming). e.g. PHASE-01_example_mvp.md
##   Gates are *sections inside* a PHASE (exit criteria), not their own domain — no `GATE` prefix exists
##   in VALID_DOMAINS, and adding a domain with zero real usage would violate the anti-bloat rule
##   (proposals/_GUIDE ⚖️). If gates ever need their own lineage, propose the domain first.
